Tips & Advice

TAWAL is the leading company in the Middle East in the field of telecommunications infrastructure and information technology, serving a wide range of customers including telecommunications service providers and others. Training with TAWAL will give you the opportunity to work with an exceptional and experienced team in various departments of TAWAL, as well as providing practical and knowledge-based experiences.

  • Visit TAWAL's website and learn about its field of work and projects.
  • Read about the field of work of the department in which you will be trained, and prepare your questions and the topics you want to learn.
  • Define your personal goals for the training period so that you can focus on them and make them a priority.
  • Identify the skills you want to develop and work on during the training period.
  • Ask your supervisor about the requirements you need to bring, such as a laptop and other necessary equipment.
  • Ask your training supervisor all your questions and try to benefit as much as possible from their experience and knowledge.
  • Take notes on what you learn, and try to summarize and link the information together to create an easy reference for everything you have learned during the training period.
  • When you learn a new skill, practice it repeatedly and read about it.
  • In addition to periodic evaluation by your training supervisor, make sure to evaluate yourself as well, as you are the one who knows your strengths and weaknesses.
  • Build good relationships in your environment and develop your communication skills during the training period.
  • Remember that discipline, time management and commitment to ethical and professional standards will achieve the maximum results from the training period.
